4月6日的笔记

By Li

发表于 2015-04-06

文章目录

Long time no see. During the Qingming holiday, I didn’t do something worth recording. On Thursday of the last week, I finished my last daily report at noon on the train. And in the evening, I participated in the test of Alibaba’s intern qualification. To be honest, I tried my best but did not solve anyone of the 3 programming quiz, although I had idea of one. It was reasonable and receivable that I might lost the chance, since I didn’t really delve into algorithm.

However, what happened the next day was undoubtedly frustrated. Because of the error made by Tencent of email classification, my invitation email was regarded as junk mail and put into the trash bin. As a result, I missed the little opportunity of working in Microsoft. It made me furious at that time, but after about 5 minutes, I settled down, and realized that it might only prevent me from daydreaming.

Then, the other 2 days, Saturday and Sunday, I worked on opencv and leap motion. It took lots of times to download the VS2013 and built the environment. After dealing with the basic staff, I eventually began coding with the API’s provided by leap motion. Admittedly, the coding style seemed fantastic, which was not only accurate but also concise. Leap motion itself, the hardware, was cool too. Although I coudln’t handle it until now, I didn’t think it was hard. In the near future, I will make it work with cv.

总之，这几天过的比较随意，基本进入放假状态。实习的事可以不用考虑了，毕竟投入有限，之前的期望也有点不切实际。微软的题目后来看了一下，估计时间充足也没法做，所以错过虽然很郁闷，但也没什么实质损失。未来几天把DRLSE的C++搞定测试一下吧。ML的课也要继续了。
