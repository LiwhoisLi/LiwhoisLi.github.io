4月7日的笔记

By Li

发表于 2015-04-07

文章目录

Today I got up at 7’o clock, and quickly had a breakfast. Then I came to the lab, starting to learn and work.

Before 10, I mainly focused on leap motion, trying to combine it with opencv lib to make a program where swiping the fingers could make a picture blurry or sharp. Although the expectation seemed good, the fact was that my leap motion couldn’t work as I thought. After times of refining, I still couldn’t solve the problem. Hence, I decided to pause, and turn to my main program.

Then I began to go on rewriting the DRLSE algorithm with C++. Last days, I attempted to customize my own class and operator, and had written part of them. However, when I eventually realized that I couldn’t load the image, I gave up all of them. After yesterday’s work, tasks need doing today was not much.

During coding, I gradually felt the merit of C++, especially the template and overload. As a skilled C programmer, I coudn’t express my excitement when I really had grasp of these features. Also, the polymorphism of class caught my attention, since it was an accurate reflection of the natural world.

In the afternoon, I attended the CPP class, learning something about the overload of operator. Despite the fact that I had already programmed with this method, I didn’t realize that I made lots of faults until today. As a class function, if it was not declared as friend, the parameters wouldn’t include the class itself because of the exist of this pointer. Such details was not easy to remember out of the classroom; therefore, I should join in more classes.

In the pre-evening and evening, I continued coding, and there were nothing else.

总之今天过得比较典型，编程中对C++的一些特性有了更好的掌握。下午上课时通过翻书也加深了以前不在乎的个别细节的了解。
