3月26日的笔记

By Li

发表于 2015-03-26

文章目录

The power cut has just happened. Today is a typical eggache day. I got up early in the morning to attend the phylosophy class, just sitting in the classroom and studying something else. For example, I perused the mathmatic book today, trying to figure out the specific definition of gradient and divergence. Meanwhile, I also focused on various kind of calculus. Contrary to my thorough understanding of them in the past, I’m kind of a tyro right now. Just as the saying goes, to stand still is to retrograde.

At the interval of the class, I left the classroom and went to the lab, and started coding. At first, I worked on rewriting the DRLSE algorithm of edge detection from MATLAB script to C, to boost the executing efficiency. That is quite a hard mission for me, since compared with the easy access to manipulating vectors and matrix of MATLAB, writing something about image in C means that I would suffer from endless loops. Although some features of C++ such as operator overload may someway reduce the difficulty, the pointers make me dizzy. Just before noon, I was tired about it, and I went out for a lunch, then slept for a while.

In the afternoon, I mainly dealt with coding problems, including graph and tree. The graph problem was an old one, which I decided to give up after numerous attemps and meditation. The other one was easier, and I finally accomplished it. However, strangely, while I had proven the correctness of my answer in local environment, I couldn’t pass the test of Hihocoder. It really made my confused, especially I paid lots of concentration on it. I solved the problem with a recursive method, which was not efficient enough in my opinion. And due to my lack of practice, I took too may times. In general, I resolved the problem of the ergodic problem.

换回中文说两句，今天被hihocoder的算法折腾得不轻。树的遍历问题还好，关键是遇到稀疏矩阵几乎必跪。看到高分答案里那些用stl库的真心感觉无奈。明日可看点用于稀疏矩阵表示的算法。今日LINUX和图像基本是废的，明日继续。
