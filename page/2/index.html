By Li

发表于 2015-03-21

开始吧

fk了网上的一个模板后，终于把个人页面搭了起来，下一步可以考虑绑定个域名了。以后的时间里，可以在这里记录一些平常学习、阅读、研究的所见所得，还可以借此学习一下网页的制作与发布。希望能坚持下来。

On the other hand, due partly to my failure of the TOEFL test, I decide to write something in English everyday to improve my writing ability. I hope that I can write something about my reading&study&research everyday, recording something that I want to memorize. Now, I pay most of my attention to image processing and linux. By reading articles and books, I want to obtain knowledge and then explore the area. That’s all for my first blog.
