4月8日的笔记

By Li

发表于 2015-04-08

Today is a busy day. Getting up at about 7, I normally had a breakfast. Then I went to the classroom, trying to take a class. However, due fully to the way our teacher taught, I left the class at the break, and went to the lab.

To continue the yesterday’s work, I ran VS2013, and coded with opencv. Because of the lack of creation of my work, I couldn’t be absolutely focused. And, since I hadn’t read the article about the method for a long time, despite my appreciation to it, I couldn’t understand it. To end the project, I held on.

At about 2 p.m, mission complete. Then, endless test and debug began. Through comparing the result of my work with the matlab counterpart, I need to correct the faults one by one. In order to refine some bizarre faults, I even had to define some new function. For example, to got the gradient of an image, I need the diffenrence of it. At first, I thought convolving an image with a sobel operator might lead to the same consequence. But it seemed I was wrong. Moreover, how to organize the function was another question. I believed that I would obtain a lot by writing a lib myself.

After dinner, I went back to the lab, went on the test works. Eventually, after eliminating the last mistake, I successfully ran the program. But to my surprise, the algorithm written in C++ wasn’t as effective as in MATLAB. I couldn’t believe what I saw since theoretically the compiling language should be more effective than interpretive ones. Hence I decided to ask the professor tomorrow.

I began to join the online test of Tencent intern recruit at 7. Although what I petitioned was background develop, there were some questions about OC,JS or JAVA included. It made me really out of sourts. Luckily enough, the following questions were more relating to C and CPP.

Just recently, I noticed that my state in Alibaba intern recruit was changed into interview scheduling, which showed that I passed the prelim.

英语已废。今日腾讯30个不定项选择着实折腾，而且考试还要开摄像头，限制切换页面。可以说考的内容让我觉得很奇怪，毕竟至少一半是完全在蒙的。剩下的选择和问答还好点，C，C++不算难吧。没想到阿里竟然过了，幸亏面试还有快1个月，可以准备一下了。

3月28日的笔记

By Li

发表于 2015-03-28

What I did today was divided by a lunch. Before it, I was focused on sorting algorithm, writing all of the algorithms I knew including insert sort, bubble sort, merge sort, quick sort, and heap sort. Although I haven’t tested them, I’m confident that I thoroughly understand all of them.

And in the afternoon, after playing cell phones for hours, I began to watch the open course provided by Stanford about Machine learning. This time, I learned naive bayes method, an introduction to neural network, and a getting started of SVM( support vector machine). Naive Bayes is, in Andrew Ng’s words, built on a strong assumption, where each of the words in a text is conditionally independent. Also, according to him, it is a practical learning algorithm. Although the numerical calculations may by someway difficult, the theorem itself is relatively easy, based on Bayes theory. Meanwhile, Andrew Ng introduced another model, which was based on multinomial distribution rather than multivariate gaussian one. This method behaved better in text detection, despite its neglect of the sequential factor.

Then he mentioned neural network, where the working mechnism was similar to logic regression, which was based on sigmoid function. And, in order to solve linearly inseperable problems, multilayer network was introduced, and also the back propagation algorithm, another name of gradient descent algorithm in a different condition. Then, it came to the distance in multidimensional problems, ending with the compare of functional and geometric margin.

To be honest, what I can do now may be only remember some terms without truely understanding them. Thus I need time to digest them. At the end, I want to state something I sadly find out today, which is the fact that maybe the real reason for my poor writing is not the low level in my English, on the other hand, it should be attributed to my inability of organizing words. I should do more writing everyday, both in Chinese and English.

今天看到神经网络的内容，正好与之前在书上看到的对应了起来，果然也印证了我对BP与GDA(Gradient descent algorithm)相类似的看法。但同时，我发现我的遗忘又开始了，即使是当初搞得挺明白的BP也是如此。上午终于把几种O(n2)和O(nlgn)的排序“遍历”了一遍，感觉有点底了。同时还看了场火箭的胜利，可喜可贺。经过几天的酝酿，一个opinion的主题逐渐在我心中形成了，趁这个周末抓紧搞出来吧，尽量双语。ML的数学部分还是有不解的，尤其是对标量求矢量偏导导，结果还能是标量的奇怪问题，应速解决。晚上乱扔了几个球，果然比去完健身房之后准不少。
